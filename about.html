Our story

Built for Botswana

Finding trustworthy help for home jobs in Botswana has always been word-of-mouth. Handled changes that — by making it easy to post a task, find a verified worker, and complete a job with full accountability on both sides.

Why we exist

The home services market needed a reset

In Gaborone, finding a reliable plumber, cleaner, or handyman has always meant asking a neighbour or scrolling through Facebook groups — with no way to verify who you're letting into your home.

Handled was built to change that. Every worker is identity-verified before they can take a single job. Every job has mandatory before and after photos. And every price is shown upfront — no negotiating on the doorstep.

We're starting in Gaborone and building from the ground up — with local workers, local prices, and a product that understands how Botswana actually works.

Trust & Safety

What happens when something goes wrong

1
Contact support within 48 hours
Email or WhatsApp us within 48 hours of the job being marked done to open a dispute.
2
We review the photos
Both the before and after photos are reviewed by our team to assess the situation fairly.
3
Mediation & resolution
We contact both parties and work toward a fair outcome. Refunds are assessed case by case.
4
Escalation
If unresolved, the case may be referred to a third party or relevant authority in Botswana.
